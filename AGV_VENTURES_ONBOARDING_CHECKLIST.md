# BRASA MEAT INTELLIGENCE
## NEW STORE ONBOARDING CHECKLIST
**SOP V3.1 - Standard Operating Procedure**

---

### PHASE 1: PRE-FLIGHT (SYSTEM SETUP) - *Done by Admin/Director*
*Time estimate: 15 minutes*

- [ ] **1. Create the Store Profile**
  - Navigate to `Company Settings` -> `Stores`.
  - Click `Add Store`, enter the Store Name (e.g., "Dallas Downtown") and Location (e.g., "TX").
- [ ] **2. Map the Master Ledger (Product Sync)**
  - Ensure the store is linked to the company's active Master Ledger of products.
  - Verify that the specific regional products (e.g., local sausage vendors) are mapped.
- [ ] **3. Apply Operational Template**
  - In `Company Settings`, select the new store.
  - Apply the standard template (e.g., "Standard 2026 Model") to instantly inject target LBS/Guest, Cost/Guest, Lunch/Dinner prices, and "Villain" tracking rules.
- [ ] **4. Account Creation**
  - Navigate to the `Admin/Users` dashboard.
  - Create the General Manager's account.
  - Set Role to `manager` and link their profile strictly to the newly created Store ID.
  - Generate a temporary password.

---

### PHASE 2: PHYSICAL SETUP (THE SHOP FLOOR) - *Done by Manager on-site*
*Time estimate: 15 minutes*

- [ ] **1. Device Provisioning (PWA Installation)**
  - Manager opens `brasa-intel.app` on their mobile device or the kitchen iPad.
  - Clicks "Add to Home Screen" to install the Offline-First PWA app.
- [ ] **2. Walk-in Cooler Mapping**
  - Manager logs in and enters the "Weekly Pulse" module.
  - Manager physically walking the cooler to ensure the digital product order matches the shelf arrangement. (If needed, Admin adjusts the sorting order in the backend).
- [ ] **3. OneDrive Integration (For AI Agent)**
  - Ensure the store's designated scanner or email forwarding rule is set to drop Sysco/Supplier invoices into the `Brasa_Invoices_Dallas` folder on OneDrive.

---

### PHASE 3: THE 30-MINUTE TRAINING (HANDS-ON) - *Led by Implementation Team*
*Time estimate: 30 minutes*

- [ ] **Minute 0-10: The Philosophy & The Dashboard**
  - Explain the difference between *Cost/Guest* and *LBS/Guest*.
  - Show them the Leaderboard (Gamification) - "You are competing with the Miami store. Don't be last."
  - Explain how the AI reads their invoices so they don't have to type them.
- [ ] **Minute 10-20: Daily Operations (Smart Prep & Waste)**
  - Demonstrate accessing the `Smart Prep` projections for the butcher.
  - Explain "The Garcia Rule": Why they must log the high-value "Villain" waste daily. Show the lockout warning.
- [ ] **Minute 20-30: The Weekly Pulse (Walk-in Cooler Test)**
  - Turn the iPad on **Airplane Mode**.
  - Walk into the cooler and do a mock inventory count of 5 items.
  - Walk out of the cooler, turn off Airplane mode, and watch the green "Synced" badge appear.
  - Force a password change and hand over the keys.

**ONBOARDING COMPLETE.** The store is now live on the Brasa network.
