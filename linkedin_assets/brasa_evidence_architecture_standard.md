### Executive Vision

All client-facing forensic reports, pilot audits, and case-study evidence sheets generated by the BRASA Governance Engine must adhere strictly to the **Operational Evidence Architecture (OEA)**. 

OEA defines a communication standard that operates on a precise balance: it must look **cinematic and premium**, yet remain **brutally factual and highly credible** (evoking the aesthetic of an *enterprise military console* or an *academic-forensic audit*). 

We must resolutely avoid "sci-fi SaaS hype," excessive neon, and marketing hyperbole. The authority of BRASA is built on cold, digital, indisputable telemetry.

---

### The 6-Stage Forensic Reporting Standard

Every BRASA audit report must structure its variance card and discrepancy findings around the following six sequential phases:

1. **INCIDENT** (The Operational Claim)
   * *Definition:* The clear statement of what the supplier invoiced/billed vs. the nominal parameters.
   * *Tone:* Objective, dry, arithmetic. 
   * *Example:* *"The distributor billed the flagship location for 13 cases of Sirloin Coulot (Picanha, Item 7152153) totaling 1,066.300 LBS, representing a catch-weight average of 82.02 LBS per case."*

2. **TELEMETRY** (The Digital Proof)
   * *Definition:* The cold, calibrated data points captured in real-time by the BRASA scale, scanner, and decision engine at the exact millisecond of delivery.
   * *Tone:* Technical, precise, database-centric. Include counts, specific weights, and compliance scores.
   * *Example:* *"Telemetry verified that exactly 10 cases (819.50 LBS) were digitally scanned and scale-logged under strict GTIN identification, registering a 76.9% process compliance score."*

3. **TEMPORAL BLINDSPOT** (The Perishable Reality)
   * *Definition:* Exposing the operational reality of protein. Raw meat is fabricated, cooked, and consumed immediately.
   * *Tone:* High-level operational awareness, firm, dramatic but grounded.
   * *Example:* *"Within hours of delivery, the boxes were ripped open, the meats fabricated by the butchers, seasoned, and served to guests. The physical boxes and barcode labels were immediately discarded."*

4. **FORENSIC BLACK HOLE** (The Retrospective Delusion)
   * *Definition:* Explaining why traditional, retrospective audits (days/weeks later via invoices or ERP logs) are a complete delusion.
   * *Tone:* Analytical, sobering, CFO-targeted.
   * *Example:* *"By the time the invoice discrepancy was flagged days later, the physical evidence had vanished. You cannot retrospectively audit a steak that has already been eaten. Because 3 cases bypassed scale telemetry via manual check-in, the store had zero physical evidence to dispute the billing."*

5. **OPERATIONAL TRUTH** (The Core Insight)
   * *Definition:* The central thesis of the BRASA philosophy. Contrast static spreadsheet accounting with dynamic floor reality.
   * *Tone:* Authoritative, bold, intellectual (styled in terminal-monospaced typography).
   * *Example:* *"`THE OPERATIONAL TRUTH: If you do not capture telemetry at the exact moment the box crosses your dock, you are not managing inventory. You are managing ancient history.`"*

6. **ENFORCEMENT RECOMMENDATION** (The Resolution Protocol)
   * *Definition:* The immediate action step. How the system (or the operator) locks down this leakage via digital gatekeeping (e.g., *The Garcia Rule*).
   * *Tone:* Prescriptive, regulatory, software-enforced.
   * *Example:* *"Immediate operational locking recommended. Prevent manual receiving overrides across all network tenants to stop margin leakage. Enforce supervisor-level override codes to resolve unverified catch-weight variances."*

### Visual Design Tokens (The Military-Premium Theme)

To maintain absolute institutional consistency, all HTML interfaces and PDF exports must strictly follow this carbon-dark, high-credibility color palette:

*   **Deep Void** (`#08080a`): Global background. Evokes clean, deep-black server racks.
*   **Ash Gray** (`#d4d4d8`): Standard body typography. Highly readable, non-glare.
*   **Carbon Border** (`#1f1f23`): Structure borders. Subtly divides widgets.
*   **Sovereign Green** (`#10b981`): Successful scale logs, verified weights, positive compliance.
*   **Alert Red** (`#ef4444`): Unscanned boxes, manual overrides, leakage alerts.
*   **AS400 Text** (`#10b981`): Terminal simulator. Evokes retro supplier invoice screens.

#### Design Guardrails (No Sci-Fi SaaS):
1.  **NO glow effects or massive drop-shadows.** The digital scales and dashboard must look like real, rugged industrial hardware interfaces, not video game HUDs.
2.  **Use absolute monospace for telemetry.** All weights, case counts, timestamps, and hashes must be rendered in `JetBrains Mono` or `Consolas` to look mathematically calculated.
3.  **The Cryptographic Stamp.** Every report must finish with a custom verification block displaying a digital trace key and a SHA-256 signature hash to reinforce institutional security.
