The Governance Visibility Gap

Most restaurant groups do not have a food cost problem alone. They have a governance visibility problem.

Traditional ERP and inventory systems reconcile invoices and gross weights, but they do not govern the operational variance that happens between receiving, fabrication, prep, and service.

At scale, small yield discrepancies become material EBITDA exposure.

14-Day Validation Protocol

Designed for immediate execution with zero disruption to existing tech stacks.

Phase 1: Targeting DAYS 0–1

Select 2 high-volume flagship units. Define protein categories, baseline reports, receiving workflow, and operational owners.

Phase 2: Baseline Audit DAYS 2–8

Run shadow audit parallel to existing systems. Measure actual yield variance, receiving discrepancies, trim loss, and inventory drift.

Phase 3: Governance Activation DAYS 9–14

Activate variance thresholds, compliance checkpoints, and manager escalation workflow. Document leakage patterns and recovery opportunities.
